Audrey Morgan Lee

Writer · Philadelphia, PA

* * *

Audrey Morgan Lee is a writer based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She holds a double B.A. in creative writing and American studies from Franklin & Marshall College.

Her work has appeared in numerous literary journals including Wax Nine, X-R-A-Y, Necessary Fiction, Okay Donkey, and Back Patio Press, among others.

She is the author of two poetry chapbooks: Disjecta Membra (Bottlecap Press, 2022) and Probably, Angels (Maverick Duck Press, 2020). Her debut short story collection, Utter Goodness, is forthcoming from Farthest Heaven in 2026.

She is a former resident at the Sundress Academy for the Arts and a two-time Best of the Net nominee. In 2020, she received the Jerome Irving Bank Short Story Prize.
