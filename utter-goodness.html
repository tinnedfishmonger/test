Utter Goodness

Short Story Collection · Farthest Heaven · 2026

* * *

Audrey Morgan Lee's debut short story collection explores the quiet devastations and small mercies of everyday life. With precision and tenderness, these stories illuminate the moments when ordinary people confront extraordinary choices.

From the intimate to the uncanny, Utter Goodness navigates landscapes of desire, grief, and the stubborn persistence of hope. Lee's prose moves with careful attention, finding beauty in the overlooked and meaning in the mundane.
